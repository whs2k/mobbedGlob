MobbedGlob

Welcome to MobbedGlob!

This project is 3 Phased MVP effort to build a ‘data union’

THE WHY

Your clicks generate lots of money for the Facebook’s and Google’s of the world and in turn they provide us with a services to connect and inform us. But you’re creating the data; shouldn’t you be able to monitize it too?

Much like the unionization of labor in the US in the late 1800s, one person on their own does not have much power. However, when combined with many, the power of a strike or values of the data becomes significant.

THE PLAN

Phase 1: Create a chrome extension that allows users to save a csv/json of their browsing histroy DOWNLOAD THE EXTENSION HERE!

Phase 2: Allow users to upload and merge their anonmyzied jsons/csvs together (TBD)
